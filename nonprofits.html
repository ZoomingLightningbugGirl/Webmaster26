    • Local Community Organizations & Resources

    Explore local nonprofit organizations, educational resources, historical groups, wellness centers, and community programs serving the Whitemarsh Township area.

    Conshohocken-Plymouth-Whitemarsh Rotary

    A local Rotary chapter focused on community service, charitable projects, leadership, and supporting local initiatives.

    Highlands Mansion & Gardens

    A historic estate and nonprofit organization preserving local history, architecture, gardens, and educational programming.

    Hope Lodge

    A historic landmark and nonprofit organization dedicated to preservation, education, and community engagement through events and tours.

    Plymouth Meeting Historical Society

    Preserves and shares the local history of Plymouth Meeting and surrounding communities through exhibits, archives, and educational outreach.

    Simon's Fund

    A nonprofit organization focused on preventing sudden cardiac arrest in children through heart screenings, awareness, and education.

    TransNet

    A regional transportation management association helping improve mobility, commuting options, and sustainability in local communities.

    Valley Forge Tourism & Convention Board

    Promotes tourism, local events, cultural attractions, and economic growth throughout Montgomery County and the Valley Forge region.

    Wheels For Wishes

    A vehicle donation charity benefiting Make-A-Wish programs and supporting children facing critical illnesses.

    Whitemarsh Art Center

    A nonprofit community art center offering classes, exhibits, workshops, and creative programs for all ages.

    Whitemarsh Foundation

    Supports environmental preservation, open space protection, and community improvement projects within Whitemarsh Township.

    William Jeanes Memorial Library

    A community library providing educational resources, technology access, events, and learning opportunities for residents of all ages.

    Colonial Business Association

    A local business organization supporting networking, economic development, and collaboration among businesses in the Colonial School District area.

    Vehicles For Veterans

    Accepts donated vehicles to help fund programs and services that support veterans and their families.

    Chestnut Hill Meals on Wheels

    Delivers meals and and does wellness checks to seniors and individuals in need throughout the local community.

    Narcotics Anonymous Meetings

    Provides recovery support groups and resources for individuals going through substance addiction through peer support and community meetings.

    Community Canopy Project

    A community environmental group focused on planting trees, expanding green spaces, and increasing environmental sustainability.

    Retreat to St. Thomas

    A program that has spiritual retreats, wellness programs, and opportunities for reflection and community connection.

    Whitemarsh Learning & Wellness Center

    Provides educational support, tutoring, wellness services, and programs designed to strengthen learning and personal growth.
